Privacy Policy

How Kijabe Adventures collects, uses, and protects your information.

What We Collect

We collect basic contact details (name, phone, email) when you inquire or book. Payment confirmations may include M-Pesa or transaction references.

How We Use It

We use your details to confirm hikes, share trip information, and provide support. We do not sell or rent your data.

Retention

Booking records are kept for accounting and safety. You can request deletion of your contact data after your trip unless we must retain it for legal reasons.

Third Parties

We use WhatsApp for communication and Google Analytics for anonymous usage metrics. Payments are handled via M-Pesa or your chosen provider; we don’t store card details.

Your Choices

Contact us to access, correct, or delete your data. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time.
