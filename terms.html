Terms & Conditions

Key terms for joining Kijabe Adventures hikes and tours.

Bookings & Payments

Slots are confirmed after payment via M-Pesa or agreed method. Please confirm availability before paying. Keep your transaction reference for verification.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel, please notify us as early as possible so we can adjust logistics. Refunds or reschedules are handled case by case depending on notice and third-party costs.

What to Bring

Appropriate footwear, water, snacks, weather-appropriate clothing, and any personal medication. We’ll share a gear list for each hike.

Safety & Fitness

Join hikes suitable to your fitness level. Inform guides of any medical conditions. Follow guide instructions; they have authority to adjust routes for safety.

Weather & Changes

Routes or schedules may change due to weather, permits, or safety considerations. We’ll communicate changes promptly and propose alternatives where possible.

Liability

Outdoor activities carry inherent risks. By joining, you accept personal responsibility for your fitness and safety. We operate with risk awareness and first-aid-trained guides.
